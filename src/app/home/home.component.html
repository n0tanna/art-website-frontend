About the Artist

Hello! I am Anna. I'm a 25 year old Canadian who started drawing around the age of 13. I bought my first drawing tablet when I was 15, and since then have been practicing digital art on and off over the years. In the year of 2021, I took up embroidery, and then in 2022 I have started sewing.

I hope that you enjoy my work, and have a great day wherever you may be!
